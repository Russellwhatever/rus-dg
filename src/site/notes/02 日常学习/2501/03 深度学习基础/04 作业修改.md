### 2.3 修改全连接层数为2层
```python
model = nn.Sequential(

    nn.Conv2d(1, 6, kernel_size=5, stride=1, padding=2),

    nn.ReLU(),

    nn.MaxPool2d(kernel_size=2, stride=2, padding=0),

    nn.Conv2d(6, 16, kernel_size=5, stride=1, padding=0),

    nn.ReLU(),

    nn.MaxPool2d(kernel_size=2, stride=2, padding=0),

    nn.Flatten(),

    nn.Linear(16 * 5 * 5, 120),

    nn.ReLU(),

    nn.Linear(120, 10)

).to(device)
```
